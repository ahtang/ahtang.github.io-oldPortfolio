Hey Addie!

Snapchat Series Graphic

Hey Addie! is a Snapchat series that my friend Andrew recently started with our other friend Adelaide while she gave us a tour through Berkeley and San Francisco. Each Snapchat episode typically involves Andrew starting his Snap with "Hey Addie!" before continuing with a random question. As the episodes continue, Adelaide is noticeably and increasingly miffed by Andrew's crazy shenanigans and inquiries.

Process

Our personal group of friends seem to really enjoy "Hey Addie!" so I decided to make a simple graphic to commemorate the series along with all of Andrew's many saved Snaps.

The light teal background is a tribute to Adelaide's favorite shade of color. I believe that text against an asymmetrical white speech bubble keeps the overall aesthetic lighthearted. After making a few variations, I needed help in deciding which typefont to use. I went with typefont - ObelixPro - after asking for feedback because it best portrayed the strong, lively exclamation of "Hey Addie!" that Andrew uses in his Snaps. I also chose a slightly darker shade of teal to make the white speech bubble slightly more prominent.

Reflection

Although a simple design, this graphic is one of my favorites because it illustrates a fun happening with a close group of friends.

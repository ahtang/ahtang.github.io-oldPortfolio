Artery

Logo Design

Artery is a mobile/tablet application, created by a fellow UCSD student, that aims to encourage social interactions between people through their own drawings. My goal was to help the creator of Artery design a logo that best represents his application as a creative, social space.

Process

Because of the application name, Artery, I wanted to incorporate a heart to play off of how the heart pumps blood to the arteries which in turn help to carry blood out towards the rest of the body through a network of vessels. Through this mobile/tablet application, people create drawings which help spread interactions and social networks out into the community. Furthermore, because of the creative aspect of the application, I wanted to incorporate a tool of creation - the pencil. With these two concepts, the heart and the pencil, I created the design for Artery.

Reflection

Looking back now, I wish I made the pencil width slightly thicker because it can be rather difficult to see the details of such a small button tile on a mobile device. However, despite this visual flaw, since this piece was one of my first illustrations for commision, I'm quite still satisfied with the end result and most importantly, my client was pleased as well!
